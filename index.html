About

Research & Projects (Under My Supervision)

Research

Student Consultation System

An intelligent platform designed to streamline academic advising and student support services. The system leverages AI to match students with appropriate advisors and provides personalized guidance based on academic performance and career goals.

Supervising: MEHEZEBIN

Project

Language Preference in RAG Systems

Exploring whether RAG systems show a tendency to prefer evidence in certain languages when multiple language sources are available. This study aims to identify any systematic bias in multilingual retrieval.

Project

Sensitivity to Linguistic Variations

Analyzing how small changes in language—such as formality, word order, or dialect—affect RAG retrieval performance. The goal is to understand how robust the system is to language variation.

Research

Blockchain Federated Learning for Road Fault Detection

A decentralized framework combining blockchain technology with federated learning to detect and report road infrastructure faults.

Co-Supervising: SAMIA AFROZ RISHA, SAMIHA ISLAM SAMI, NUSRAT JAHAN, DIPANWITA BARUA

Project

Impact of Code-Switching on Retrieval

Investigating how mixed-language (code-switched) inputs influence the stability and reliability of RAG retrieval. This work aims to evaluate how well RAG handles multilingual content in a single query.
